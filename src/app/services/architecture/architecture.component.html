Construction Observation

Architectural construction observation is the process of monitoring and inspecting a construction site to ensure that the construction is being carried out in compliance with the architectural design plans and specifications.
This process involves regular site visits by an architect or architectural team to assess the progress of the construction work, identify any potential issues or problems, and ensure that the project is meeting all safety, quality, and legal requirements.
