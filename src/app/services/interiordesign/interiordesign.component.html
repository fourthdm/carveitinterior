C) Coordination With Architects And Contractors :





Coordination with architects and contractors is an essential aspect of the design and construction process. Architects are responsible for creating the overall design of the building or structure, while contractors are responsible for executing the construction work according to the design specifications.
Interior designers often work closely with both architects and contractors to ensure that their interior design plans are integrated seamlessly into the overall building design and construction process. .
