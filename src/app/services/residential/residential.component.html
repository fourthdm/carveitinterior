RESIDENTIAL DESIGNS



A) Residential Architecture :


Residential architecture is the branch of architecture that deals with the design and construction of residential buildings such as houses, apartments, and condominiums. It involves designing buildings that meet the specific needs and preferences of homeowners, while also complying with building codes and regulations.



B) Residential Interior :



Residential interior design is the art and science of creating functional, comfortable, and aesthetically pleasing living spaces within residential buildings. It involves designing interiors that meet the specific needs and preferences of homeowners, while also incorporating elements of style and beauty.
